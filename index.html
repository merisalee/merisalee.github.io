TanzaNumbers

For our fourth year engineering design project, our group worked with a non-profit organization, My Oral Village to design and develop a suite of Android games to teach numeracy skills to people in Tanzania. Innumeracy has extremely direct consequences on the cash economy and a person's wealth and we ensured our designs were sensitive to our target audience.

I acted as the designer and developer, designing assets in Illustrator as well as developing in Android Studio.

FoodMe

This was a project myself and 3 classmates did for our final Info Systems and Design project. We created a web app that recommends food based on time, desires and price. We wanted an app for students like us that have troubles finding places to eat. Think of it as a Songza but for food!

Requirements for the project included data flow diagrams, normalization tables and other fun stuff.
